It was fun to pick wild blackberries on the countryside last week! The flavour goes so well together with the carrots, cinnamon, nutmeg and vanilla from Madagascar üç∞ #loveirishfood #dublinfoodie
RECIPE
INGREDIENTS
Sponge: 250g Wholemeal Organic Odlums flour 1 tsp Baking Powder 1 tsp Baking Soda 2 tsp Cinnamon 1 tsp Nutmeg 9 oz (250g) Grated Carrot 200g Brown Sugar = 240gr Maple syrup 2 Flax 'Eggs' (2 tbsp of powdered flax seeds mixed with 3 tbsp of water, leave it to set for 10 minutes) ¬Ω cup (80ml) Sunflower oil 1 tsp Vanilla 1 Tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar or lemon juice 1 cup Chopped Walnuts
Prepare a 7" cake tray Mix together all the ingredients except for the flour, baking powder and baking soda which you will add spoon by spoon when the other ingredients are mixed. Bake for 40 minutes at 180C, test the cake with a chopstick before switching the oven off to make sure it is completely cooked.
Blackberry Jam Filling 3 Cups of Wild Blackberries 2 Apples chopped in small cubes Bring to boil for 30 minutes and let it cool down.
Lemon sauce 1 Tin of Organic Coconut Milk (only the solid part) 4 tbsp of icing sugar 1 tsp Vanilla 2 tsp lemon juice 1/2 Cup Chopped Pistachios
Prepare the sauce by placing the above ingredients in a mixer. Do not over mix to avoid the coconut to separate. If that happens worm it up at 25-30 C and cool down again. Once the cake is completely cooled down, spread the sauce on top and spread the pistachio on top of it.
